About Solap Agencies

Solap Agencies is a real estate services firm situated in Kiserian town, Ngong division of Kajiado County, Kenya. We specialize in sourcing land for our clients for both purchase or leasing. Our clients include church organizations, SACCOs, and individuals.

We also sell lands and built-up properties on behalf of our clients and provide property management services for apartments, etc. Furthermore, we advise our clients on insurance coverage to protect against potential losses of fixed and movable assets, stocks, and personal health.

Our mission is to provide the best service to achieve customer satisfaction. Our motto: Service with Integrity.
